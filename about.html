Enchanted Escapes Await! Step into a magical 16th-century manor where dreams meet reality.

A Whimsical Warwickshire Wonderland!

Twirl into 65 acres of enchanted countryside at Sunday Warwickshire Walton Hall Hotel & Spa! This magical hideaway weaves timeless charm with modern sparkle, just a wand’s flick from Warwick and Stratford-upon-Avon. Prepare for a spellbinding adventure!

A 16th-Century Enchanted Manor!

Wander into the Grade II-listed Walton Hall, a 16th-century manor glowing with history since the Mordaunt family’s reign! With twinkling fireplaces, whimsical ceilings, and grand staircases, this manor is charmed to preserve its ancient allure. Every corner whispers a magical tale!

Charming Rooms, Magical Vibes!

Drift into the contemporary Walton Hotel for a sprinkle of stylish serenity! From luxurious rooms in the Hall to dazzling suites with countryside views, your enchanted escape is woven with retro charm and modern magic!

Spellbinding Wellness!

Dive into enchantment at our Spa & Leisure Club! Splash in the shimmering pool, bask in the sauna, or indulge in one of seven mystical therapy rooms. Wellness, beauty, and tranquility dance together in a magical symphony!

Dining That Casts a Spell!

Savor the magic at Walton! From the glass-fronted Orangery Restaurant to the cozy Conservatory Bar, our chefs conjure fresh, seasonal delights with a local twist. Sip, dine, and gaze at lakeside vistas—pure culinary enchantment!

Celebrations with Fairy-Tale Flair!

Dream of a magical wedding, a mystical business event, or a festive Christmas gala? Our Moncrieffe Suite, Courtyard Suite, and historic St James chapel weave unforgettable moments with whimsical charm!

Heartwarming Magic & Hospitality!

At Walton, you’re part of the fairy tale! Our team sprinkles magic on every stay—whether a romantic retreat, a grand wedding, or a lively business event. True luxury is feeling at home with a touch of enchantment!

Explore, Dream, Celebrate!

Bring your furry friend to our dog-friendly rooms or venture to Stratford-upon-Avon, Warwick Castle, and the Cotswolds. With a relaxing spa, free parking, and on-site dining, your magical escape is near the M40 and Birmingham International Airport. Let’s weave an epic tale!

Getting Here

From the M40 (North), exit at junction 13. Turn left and then take the first right. Continue through Newbold Pacey, then turn left at the T-junction in Wellesbourne.

From the M40 (South), exit at junction 15. Take the fourth exit onto the A429 to Cirencester. Continue through Barford and turn left at the roundabout for Wellesbourne.
