Welcome to Walton Hall

The 16th Century grade 2 listed, four-star Walton Hall and the beautiful modern Walton Hotel are set in 65 acres of glorious countryside overlooking a lake yet, is also within easy reach of the historic towns of Warwick and Stratford-upon-Avon. Just 10 minutes from the M40, and easily accessible from Birmingham International Airport.

Walton Hall's secluded setting and extensive facilities make it one of the region's most versatile and desirable venues for corporate and private events.

Spa & Leisure

In the tranquil Warwickshire countryside, the Spa sits on the Walton Hall estate, while the health and leisure club is part of the more contemporary part of the property. Offering everything a modern traveller could ask for to work out and wind down, there's a fitness studio, an indoor pool, sauna and steam room, as well as views onto the pretty gardens and grounds outside.

In addition to the facilities, the spa offers a range of face and body treatments, manicures and pedicures. There are seven treatment rooms, and therapists perfect for pampering and feeling your best.

Getting Here

From the M40 (North), exit at junction 13. Turn left and then take the first right. Continue through Newbold Pacey, then turn left at the T-junction in Wellesbourne.

From the M40 (South), exit at junction 15. Take the fourth exit onto the A429 to Cirencester. Continue through Barford and turn left at the roundabout for Wellesbourne.
